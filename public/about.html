About Me

I am an incredibly curious multi-passionate person whose eyes for novelty are sometimes bigger than my stomach. I love trying new things, ending up in new places, and starting new hobbies.

On this website, you will find the fruits of my latest passion, web development. I studied Computer Science in college, but because I am more of a scientist of people than binary numbers, I opted to get my minor in it. Over a decade later, I decided to revisit coding one more time, just to see if I really didn't like it, or my environment in college had tainted my view of it.

So far, so good! Join me as I engage with my coding, and other projects. I can't wait to share my journey with you!
